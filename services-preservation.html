Property Preservation Services

At Haven Property Care, we specialize in property preservation for upscale residences across Florida. Our services are fully customized to meet the exact needs and expectations of each homeowner, ensuring your property receives the highest level of care even when you're away.

Home Watch Services

Regular visual inspections of your property's interior and exterior. We check for leaks, pest issues, security concerns, and weather damage while you're away.

Storm Preparation

Pre-hurricane inspections and preparation. We secure outdoor items, check shutters, and ensure your property is ready for severe weather before you return.

Vendor Coordination

We manage trusted local contractors for repairs, maintenance, and improvements. You approve all work. We handle the scheduling and supervision.

Security Monitoring

Peace of mind with regular security checks. We verify alarms, locks, and surveillance systems are functioning properly while you're away.
